I am a PhD student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at UIUC advised by Ravishankar K. Iyer in the DEPEND research group. I am broadly interested in improving performance and reliability of networked and distributed systems. My recent work has focused on designing application-aware network resource management for high-performance computing systems. I am also interested in exploring emerging datacenter paradigms like memory disaggregation and serverless computing.

Upcoming summer, I will intern at Google. Previously, I have interned at IBM Research.
